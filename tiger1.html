Tiger I

When people think of tnaks and World War 2, the Tiger I is usually the first tank to come to mind. Although historically unreliable and overengineered, the Tiger I was one of the most feared tank for the Allies to handle. The fears were justified, with over 100mm of armor on the front and a ferocious 88mm cannon that destroyed Allied tanks in one shot. Several accounts exist of single Tigers taking on several opponents. Not many of these beasts were made, but you can own this fury, redesigned and improved, today!
