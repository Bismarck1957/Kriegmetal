About Us

Humble Origins...

The company started as a small workshop idea, originally built as a metalshop based on building tanks for the civilian use. The idea then has changed to build tanks for use in airsoft and paintball matches, promoting safety as the top policy in making our tanks. With this in mind, we built our shop fresh out of college, our dream never dying away.

...To an Industrial Might

Today, our dream lives on, and we are exanding rapidly. Our manufacturing lines are top of the line, all handcrafted by expert machinests. Our steel and engines are hand selected from trusted US dealers.
